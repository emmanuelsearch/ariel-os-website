Ariel OS is an operating system for secure, memory-safe, low-power Internet of Things (IoT). It is written in Rust and supports hardware based on 32-bit microcontroller architectures (Cortex-M, RISC-V, and Xtensa). Check it out on [Github](https://github.com/ariel-os/ariel-os).
